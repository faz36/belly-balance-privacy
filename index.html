Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Belly Balance ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Belly Balance:

  • Email address (for account registration)
  • Personal health information (age, weight, height, trimester, due date)
  • Medical conditions (optional)
  • Nutrition and food intake logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide personalized nutrition recommendations during pregnancy
  • Track and display your nutrition history
  • Improve our app and services

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which complies with industry-standard security practices. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

4. Google Sign-In

If you choose to sign in using Google, we receive your name and email address from Google. This information is used solely for account creation and identification purposes.

5. Children's Privacy

Belly Balance is designed for pregnant women and is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
